Lattice QCD Flavour Physics Nonperturbative SM Numerical Methods

Research

I develop and apply lattice-QCD methods to obtain first-principles predictions for flavour observables dominated by long-distance QCD. My work focuses on rare decays and mixing amplitudes, and on new techniques that extend the reach of nonperturbative computations.
